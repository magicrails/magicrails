# Code of Conduct

Magicrails follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

We want this project to be a productive place for everyone who shows up to contribute, regardless of background or experience level. That means:

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, reviews, and chat.
- Assume good faith. If you think someone is being unkind, ask before reacting.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- If you make a mistake, own it and move on. Everyone does.

## Not welcome

- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained disruption of discussion.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Posting other people's private information without their permission.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or low-effort bait posts.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that breaks this code, contact the maintainers privately at **masketir84000@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed and acted on as quickly as the maintainers can manage. Reporters' identities will be kept confidential.

Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, ban accounts, or take other actions they consider proportionate to the situation.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces — the GitHub repo, the issue tracker, discussions, the Discord server, and any other forum that is officially part of the project. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public.
